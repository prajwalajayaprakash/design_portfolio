Meet the team

Iain Shovlin

Chairman & Founding Partner


Chicago, USA

Finance is critical. If sufficient investment is made in infrastructure and venture capital is made available, there will be a big improvement in the situation.

Before forming Guild Capital in 2009, Iain was a venture capitalist with Technology Crossover Ventures in Palo Alto, California for six years. At TCV he undertook investments spanning consumer internet, business process outsourcing, and software. Prior to venture capital, Iain was a management consultant with Bain & Company.

Iain was born in Glasgow, Scotland and has an M.A. in International Business from the University of Edinburgh, Scotland.
